◆ UUID_BROADCAST_SERVICE

#define UUID_BROADCAST_SERVICE   0xFE8F

The GATT Broadcast Service, as supplied, uses a 16-bit UUID purchased by Qualcomm for the Service UUID. This 16-bit UUID may be used by our customers on condition that the service and its implementation in the gatt_broadcast_client and gatt_broadcast_server libraries and sink application shall not be modified by the customer. Alternatively, if the customer wishes to customize the service, a new service UUID may be purchased from the Bluetooth SIG and shall be used in place of our 16-bit UUID; see "https://www.bluetooth.com/specifications/assigned-numbers/16-bit-uuids-for-members"
